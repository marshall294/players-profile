Riders FC Players Code of Conduct

1. Training

All players must attend training sessions on time as scheduled by the coaching staff.

Players are expected to give their full effort during training and maintain a positive attitude.

Respect the coaching staff's decisions and follow their instructions without argument.

2. Inter-Player Relationships

Treat all teammates with respect, regardless of their skills, background, or position.

Avoid conflicts and work towards fostering a supportive and inclusive environment.

Resolve disputes amicably or with the help of a coach or team manager.

3. Keeping Club Information Confidential

Players must not share any club-related information, strategies, or plans with outsiders.

Discussions regarding sensitive matters within the club must stay within the team.

Any breaches of confidentiality will be taken seriously and may result in disciplinary action.

4. Obeying Rules and Regulations

All players are required to adhere to the club’s rules, as well as league and governing body regulations.

Any form of misconduct, including the use of banned substances, violent behavior, or disrespect to officials, will not be tolerated.

5. Complete Training Kit

Players must come to every training session and match with the full and proper training kit as specified by the club.

Kits must be clean and maintained to the club's standards.

6. Skip Match or Training Permission

Players must notify the coach or team manager at least 24 hours in advance if they are unable to attend a match or training session, unless it’s an emergency.

Valid reasons for absence (e.g., illness, family emergencies) must be communicated promptly.

7. Personal Development

Players are encouraged to take responsibility for their personal growth, including physical fitness, mental well-being, and skill development.

Use resources provided by the club, such as training programs and workshops, to improve both on and off the field.

8. Training Habits

Players are expected to develop positive training habits, including consistency, punctuality, and focus.

Continually strive to improve your skills and fitness levels through dedicated effort during every training session.

9. Playing for Other Clubs Without Permission

Players must not participate in matches or training with other clubs without prior written consent from the Riders FC coaching staff or management.

Playing for other teams without permission could result in suspension or termination of the player's contract with the club.

10. Respect for Club Property

Players must treat all club property, including training equipment, facilities, and team gear, with respect and care.

Any damage to club property due to negligence or inappropriate use may result in disciplinary action and potential fines.

11. Representing Riders FC

Players are expected to represent Riders FC with professionalism and respect at all times, both on and off the field.

This includes maintaining a positive public image and behaving in a manner that reflects the values of the club.

12. Drug and Alcohol Policy

The use of illegal substances, alcohol, or performance-enhancing drugs is strictly prohibited at all times, both during and outside of club activities.

Violations will be dealt with severely and may result in suspension or termination of the player's involvement with the club.

13. Communication and Accountability

Players are expected to maintain open lines of communication with coaches, team managers, and other relevant club officials.

Be accountable for your actions and take responsibility for any mistakes or shortcomings in your conduct.

14. Disciplinary Action

Failure to adhere to the Riders FC Players Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary actions, ranging from warnings to suspension or termination of membership from the club.

The severity of the action will be based on the nature and frequency of the violation.
